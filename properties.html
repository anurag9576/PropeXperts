Mandate

A mandate in real estate refers to an exclusive or non-exclusive agreement between a property owner and a real estate agency to market, promote, and sell or lease the property within a defined timeframe. The mandate outlines the agent’s responsibilities, commission structure, and terms of service. Through this arrangement, the agency strategically markets the property, attracts genuine buyers or tenants, and ensures a smooth transaction process. Mandate work is crucial in real estate as it builds trust, streamlines operations, and delivers value to both clients and agents.
